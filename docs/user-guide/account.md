# Account Settings

The **Account Settings** page lets every user update their own profile information. No special role is required; each user can only modify their own account.

Navigate to your account by clicking your username or avatar in the top navigation bar and choosing **Account Settings** (or **Profile**).

---

## Changing your name

1. Locate the **Name** section.
2. Enter your new full name.
3. Click **Save**.
4. 
The new name appears immediately in the navigation bar.

---

## Changing your email address

Your email address is your login identifier. To change it:

1. Locate the **Email** section.
2. Enter your **current email** (for confirmation).
3. Enter the **new email** address.
4. Enter the new email again in the **Confirm new email** field.
5. Click **Save**.

!!! warning
    After saving, your login username changes to the new email address. Use the new address on your next login.

---

## Changing your password

1. Locate the **Password** section.
2. Enter your **current password**.
3. Enter the **new password**.
4. Enter the new password again in the **Confirm new password** field.
5. Click **Save**.

Password requirements:

- Minimum length is enforced by the backend (check the error message for the exact requirement).
- Avoid reusing recent passwords.
- After saving, subsequent logins require the new password.

!!! tip
    If you were given a temporary password by an administrator, change it on your first login using this form.

---

## Session management

Changes to email and password take effect for new login sessions. If you are logged in on multiple devices, those sessions will remain active until they expire or you log out manually.

To log out of the current session, click your username in the top navigation bar and choose **Log out**.
